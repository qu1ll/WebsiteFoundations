Jalapeno Poppers

Description

Baked jalapeno poppers stuffed with Cheddar cheese, cream cheese, and panko bread crumbs for added crunch. Baking them is so much easier than frying them!

Ingredients

  • 12 Jalapeno Peppers, Halved Lengthwise and Seeded
  • 2 Ounces of Cream Cheese, Softened
  • 2 Ounces of Shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1/4 Cup of Panko Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray.
  2. Fill each jalapeno pepper half with cream cheese, Cheddar cheese, and bread crumbs, respectively; arrange on the prepared baking sheet
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until jalapenos are tender and cheese melts, about 20 minutes.
